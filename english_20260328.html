Part 2. 오늘의 단어장

주 후반(목~일) 미션: 단어 씹어먹기!

musical
related to music or making music, or someone who is good at music.
👉 My sister is very musical and plays many instruments.
awesome
very impressive, great, or wonderful.
👉 The concert was totally awesome!
guitar
a musical instrument with strings, played by picking or strumming.
👉 He learned to play the guitar when he was young.
perform
to sing, dance, or act in front of people.
👉 The students will perform a special dance tonight.
band
a group of people who play musical instruments together, often for concerts.
👉 My favorite band is playing a show next month.
exciting
making you feel very happy and interested; thrilling.
👉 The roller coaster ride was very exciting!
regularly
often, or at the same time each day, week, or month.
👉 I visit my grandparents regularly, usually every Sunday.
instrument
a tool or device used for a specific purpose, especially one that makes music.
👉 The piano is a large musical instrument.
